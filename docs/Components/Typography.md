A well-designed landing page is a good starting place to welcome users into your developer hub. This is the page we recommend you customize the most.

If you're only using the Guides (also known as Documentation) feature, you can just direct your users to that on the first page in your [site navigation](https://docs.readme.com/main/docs/site-navigation).

**Promo Title**<br />This will be the title text that goes into header.

**Promo Text**<br />This will be the text that goes underneath the promo title, but it will still display in the header.

**Content**<br />Underneath the promo title and text, you will have the option of having Nothing, Buttons, Search Bar, or Custom HTML. Custom HTML will override your promo title/text, so keep that in mind when editing.
